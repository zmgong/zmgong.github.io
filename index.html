ZeMing Gong

I am a Master student at Simon Fraser University. I completed my undergraduate studies at SFU as well, majoring in Computing Science. I am actively engaged in research within the GrUVi Lab and supervised by Angel Xuan Chang. My research interests span the fields of computer vision and natural language processing. Specifically, I focus on leveraging neural networks to learn and extract genetic information
